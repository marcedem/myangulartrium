About Artrium

Artrium is a community platform that champions the work of young and aspiring artists and encourages a younger audience to experience, discuss and interact with art, both online via our mobile app, and offline in our events and exhibitions.


Unlike the current art market, where the opinions of a few influential players decide which artists are promoted, Artrium incorporates audience opinion into the curating process.


By promoting young talent and engaging with diverse, global audiences, Artrium establishes a market place specifically for young and aspiring artists. Artrium aims to help artists take vital steps on their path to a career, therefore closing the gap between local, limited-capacity galleries and big, anonymous online sale platforms.
